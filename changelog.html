Screenlapse 1.02026-06-21

First public release.

Added

  • Scheduled screen capture on a configurable interval, delivered to a folder you choose — a NAS share or any local folder.
  • Reliable delivery: each frame is written to your Mac first, then copied; if the destination goes offline, captures queue locally and upload automatically when it returns.
  • Capture all displays separately, a single display, or one stitched panorama — as HEIC, JPEG, or PNG.
  • Privacy controls: auto-pause while chosen apps (password managers, banking, …) are frontmost, a global pause hotkey, retention limits, and a free-disk floor.
  • Native menu-bar app with a live countdown and capture count, a guided first-run welcome, and a tabbed Settings window.
  • Launch at login and automatic, signed updates.
Updates are delivered automatically in‑app · screenlapse.net
